Richard David James (born 18 August 1971), primarily known as Aphex Twin, is a pioneering electronic musician and composer whose innovative approach has revolutionized modern electronic music.

Beginning in the late 1980s and throughout the 1990s, James released groundbreaking works that would define genres like IDM (Intelligent Dance Music), ambient techno, and experimental electronic music.
